Engineer Assitant Internship

Saint-Emilion, France - May to July 2020

Software development for micro-robotics at Vitirover to detect mechanical malfunction through audio signals.Vitirover is a small French company which offers grass management services using small mower-robots, mainly to wine producer near Bordeaux and to others companies (SNCF, EDF, ..) in France and in Europe. My work was to try and create a software capable of recognizing mechanical malfunctions on the robots. To do so, I make great use of FFT (Fast Fourier Transform) to analyse audio signal in real-time, and decide whether or not someone need to intervene on the robot. When a malfunction is detected, a message is sent to a technician who can now go and fix the problem. This solution allows to ensure maintenance remotely without having someone on the field all the time.
